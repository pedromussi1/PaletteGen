Free Online Color Palette Generator

PaletteGen is a free color palette generator that helps designers, developers, and artists create beautiful color schemes instantly. Whether you need a color palette for your website, app, brand identity, or illustration, PaletteGen has you covered.

How to Use

  • Harmony Mode — Pick a base color and choose a color harmony type (analogous, complementary, triadic, split-complementary, tetradic, or monochromatic) to generate palettes based on color theory.
  • Random Mode — Generate visually pleasing random palettes using golden ratio hue distribution.
  • Image Mode — Upload any image to extract its dominant colors using k-means color clustering.
  • Lock Colors — Click the lock icon on any swatch to keep it while regenerating the rest of the palette.
  • Press Space to quickly generate a new palette.

Export Options

Export your color palette as CSS custom properties (CSS variables), a Tailwind CSS config snippet, or download as an SVG swatch file. Click any color swatch to copy its hex code. Share your palette instantly via the URL — the colors are encoded in the link.

Color Harmony Types

  • Analogous — Colors adjacent on the color wheel, creating a smooth, cohesive look.
  • Complementary — Colors opposite each other on the wheel, offering high contrast.
  • Triadic — Three colors evenly spaced (120° apart) for vibrant, balanced palettes.
  • Split Complementary — A base color plus two colors adjacent to its complement.
  • Tetradic — Four colors forming a rectangle on the wheel, for rich color variety.
  • Monochromatic — Variations of a single hue at different lightness and saturation levels.

All processing happens in your browser — no images or data are uploaded to any server. Works on desktop and mobile.
